About me

My name is Gian Federspiel. I am a 17 year old student, living and working out of Lucerne, Switzerland. At the moment I am in the second year of my apprenticeship as a computer scientist. In my free time I like to devote myself to sports or drawing.

Gf archive is a page made to get a web view of the @gf archive Instagram account. With the content I try to show my sources of inspiration of art, architecture and fashion. The creation of the website served additionally the repetition of webdesign, which is a part of my education as a computer scientist.

In the future I will show my personal projects, concerning fashion and computer science on this site.
